What is Wallpaper Share Event? and how to Join

Wallpaper Share Event is a community event held by animepict9, where participants will collect wallpapers according to the given theme and quest.

What is the difference between General Design and Anime?

Both sections are part of the same creative hub. Anime Graphic Design Related focuses on anime-themed works (posters, fanart, wallpapers), while General Graphic Design Related covers broader designs (business cards, flyers, logos). Together they let you share and explore all kinds of graphic design

What is the focus of the “Anime Graphic Design Related”

Is focused on sharing anime-themed designs such as posters, covers, logos, fanart, edits, wallpapers, banners, and other creative works

Who can join this community?

Anyone interested in graphic design — whether anime-themed or general design — is welcome to join, share their work, and get inspired.

What are the benefits of joining?

Showcase your creative work, Gain fresh ideas and inspiration, Learn from others work and discussions, Unleash and grow your creativity in design, and Many More!
